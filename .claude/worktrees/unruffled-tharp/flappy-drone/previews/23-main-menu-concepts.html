Main Menu — Layout Concepts

Three layout approaches for the game's main menu. All use 620x640 canvas with live scene behind.

CHOICE Pick your preferred layout

1 / Concept A — "Top Heavy" RECOMMENDED
Layout: Title + slogan in upper third. Mode and drone pickers stacked cleanly in the middle zone with section labels. Drone renders large below the pickers with clear space around it. CTA at bottom. Everything centred, lots of breathing room. The drone is the visual anchor — sits alone with space above and below.
Pros: Clean hierarchy, title is prominent, drone has room to breathe, pickers don't overlap the drone.
Cons: Title area may feel empty on small screens.
2 / Concept B — "Split Screen"
Layout: Title sits at the very top, smaller. Drone dominates the left-centre of the screen at hero scale. Mode + drone pickers stack on the right side, vertically centred. CTA centred at bottom. Creates an asymmetric, dynamic feel — the drone is the star, controls are accessible but don't compete.
Pros: Feels like a character select screen, drone has maximum presence.
Cons: Asymmetric layout may feel unbalanced, text harder to read near city backdrop.
3 / Concept C — "Minimal Bottom Bar"
Layout: Title large and centred in the upper half. Drone floats in open sky with just stars and moon behind it — maximum atmosphere. All controls compressed into a dark translucent bar at the bottom (mode | drone | start), leaving the rest of the canvas clean and cinematic.
Pros: Most atmospheric, game scene is the hero, controls are compact and out of the way.
Cons: Controls may feel cramped, less discoverable for first-time players.
